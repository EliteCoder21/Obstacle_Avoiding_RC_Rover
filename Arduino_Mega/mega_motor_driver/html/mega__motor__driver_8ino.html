Detailed Description

Arduino Mega motor driver for the RC rover.

Listens for motion commands sent from the ESP32 over Serial1 and controls two DC motors through the Adafruit Motor Shield (V2). Commands include: FORWARD, BACKWARD, LEFT, RIGHT, and STOP.

Motors:

  • M1 = leftMotor
  • M2 = rightMotor
